# Spice Grill & Bar - AI Documentation

## Overview
Spice Grill & Bar is a premier Indian restaurant located in Ash Fork, Arizona, specializing in authentic Punjabi cuisine. We blend traditional flavors with modern culinary excellence, featuring clay oven (Tandoor) cooking and over 25 signature spices. We are 100% Halal certified.

## Menu Highlights
Our menu features a wide range of traditional dishes:
- **Appetizers**: Samosas, Pakoras, Chicken Tikka, Tandoori Wings.
- **Tandoori Specials**: Tandoori Chicken, Paneer Tikka.
- **Curries (Veg & Non-Veg)**: Butter Chicken, Chicken Tikka Masala, Goat Curry, Lamb Vindaloo, Dal Makhani, Saag Paneer, Chana Masala, Shahi Paneer, and Paneer Masala.
- **Breads**: Freshly baked Naan (Garlic, Butter, Cheese) and Roti.
- **Rice & Biryani**: Chicken, Goat, Lamb, and Veg Biryanis.
- **Desserts**: Gulab Jamun, Kheer, Mango Kulfi.
